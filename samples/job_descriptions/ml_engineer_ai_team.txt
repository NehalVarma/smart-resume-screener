# Machine Learning Engineer - AI Team

## Company: DataVision AI

### About the Role

We're looking for a talented **Machine Learning Engineer** to join our AI team. You will work on cutting-edge machine learning projects, including natural language processing, computer vision, and recommendation systems.

### What You'll Do

- Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models at scale
- Build and optimize ML pipelines for training and inference
- Work with large datasets and implement data preprocessing workflows
- Collaborate with data scientists to productionize ML models
- Implement MLOps best practices and monitoring
- Research and experiment with new ML techniques and algorithms
- Optimize model performance and reduce inference latency
- Create APIs for model serving

### Required Skills & Experience

- 3+ years of experience in machine learning or data science
- Strong programming skills in **Python**
- Hands-on experience with ML frameworks: **TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn**
- Experience with deep learning architectures (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers)
- Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms and statistics
- Experience with model deployment and serving
- Proficiency with data manipulation libraries (Pandas, NumPy)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Strong understanding of software engineering principles
- Experience with version control (Git)

### Preferred Qualifications

- Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
- Experience with **NLP** or **Computer Vision**
- Knowledge of **LLMs** (GPT, BERT, etc.) and Hugging Face Transformers
- Experience with MLOps tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, SageMaker)
- Familiarity with Apache Spark or distributed computing
- Experience with A/B testing and experimentation
- Knowledge of model optimization and quantization
- Publications in ML conferences or journals
- Experience with real-time inference systems
- Proficiency with SQL and NoSQL databases
