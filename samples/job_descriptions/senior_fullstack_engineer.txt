# Senior Full Stack Engineer

## Company: Tech Innovators Inc.

### About Us
We are a fast-growing technology company building next-generation SaaS products. Our platform serves over 1 million users worldwide.

### Job Description

We are seeking an experienced **Senior Full Stack Engineer** to join our engineering team. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and deploying scalable web applications.

### Responsibilities
- Design and develop full-stack web applications using modern technologies
- Build and maintain RESTful APIs and microservices
- Collaborate with product managers and designers to implement new features
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
- Optimize application performance and scalability
- Mentor junior developers and participate in code reviews
- Participate in architectural decisions and technical planning
- Deploy and maintain applications in cloud environments

### Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in **Python** and **JavaScript**
- Experience with modern frontend frameworks (React, Vue.js, or Angular)
- Solid understanding of backend development (Node.js, Django, or Flask)
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Experience with Docker and containerization
- Strong understanding of RESTful API design
- Experience with Git and version control
- Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills

### Nice to Have
- Experience with TypeScript
- Knowledge of microservices architecture
- Experience with GraphQL
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines
- Experience with NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Redis)
- Knowledge of Kubernetes
- Experience with testing frameworks (Jest, Pytest)
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

- Remote work options
- Professional development budget
- Modern tech stack and tools

- Frontend: React, TypeScript, Redux, Next.js
